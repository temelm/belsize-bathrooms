About us

Belsize Bathrooms have over 30 years experience in the bathroom industry.

We offer a complete package for your bathroom which includes design, supply, and installation.

Our aim is to provide our customers with a friendly informative service whether we are suppling a soap dish or a complete installation.

Our showroom provides the latest in designs from a host of leading manufacturers and designers and we would be happy to talk to you about your projects.

Hope to see you soon.
